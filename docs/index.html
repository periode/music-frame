Tonkasten
a generative sonic atmosphere contained in a wooden frame.

turn it on, and let it fill the room for as long as needed.

    sounds

    the soundscape loaded on the frame are played back dynamically. here are examples for the different modes of composition.

  • random delayed
    each sample plays back one after the other, picked randomly. a break of random duration is inserted after each sample.
    ready to play
  • markov chain
    each sample has a defined probability to play a given sample next. this allows for a more defined structure.
    in progress
  • polyphonic
    up to three samples can play at the same time, either randomly or in a markov chain.
    in progress
